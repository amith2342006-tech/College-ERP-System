# Features

# College ERP System

The College ERP System is designed to simplify and automate academic and administrative processes through the following features.

---

## Authentication & Authorization

* Secure user login and logout
* Role-based access control (Admin, Faculty, Student)
* Password encryption
* Session management

---

## Student Management

* Add, update, and delete student records
* View student profiles
* Manage student enrollment
* Search and filter students

---

## Faculty Management

* Add, update, and delete faculty records
* Assign faculty to departments and courses
* View faculty profiles

---

## Department & Course Management

* Create and manage departments
* Add and manage courses
* Assign subjects to departments
* Manage academic semesters

---

## Attendance Management

* Mark daily attendance
* View attendance history
* Calculate attendance percentage
* Generate attendance reports

---

## Marks & Examination Management

* Upload internal and external marks
* View student results
* Calculate grades
* Generate mark sheets

---

## Timetable Management

* Create class schedules
* View student timetable
* View faculty timetable
* Update timetable information

---

## Fee Management

* Maintain student fee records
* Track payment status
* View pending fees
* Generate fee reports

---

## Notification System

* Publish announcements
* Send important notices
* Display notifications on the dashboard

---
